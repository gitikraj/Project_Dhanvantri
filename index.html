Dhanvantri ID is a 14 digit unique number used to identify people, authenticate them, and thread their health records (only with their consent) across multiple healthcare service providers. Creating an ID is a must for users who want to have their health records stored digitally on the website.

What is a
Dhanvantri
ID ?

Dhanvantri number Is a unique 14 digit number to identify a person and update their health records across multiple health service providers. PHR address or Dhanvantri  address is created along with the Dhanvantri  number during registration.



Dhanvantri  address is a self-declared username just like the email address & is used to sign into the Health Information Exchange & consent manager. PHR app / Health Locker: Is used to receive, store & share medical records between patients & healthcare service providers.

How to create Dhanvantri card or Health ID Card?

Individuals can create their Dhanvantri  in 3 easy steps:

Your Dhanvantri card is created. You can see it in your profile

Enter the Aadhar number and verify it with OTP

Choose Dhanvantri  address and Confirm & your Dhanvantri  card has been created

Provide consent for the website & your account is ready to receive & store health records
